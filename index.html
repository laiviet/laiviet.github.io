About

Hi I'm Viet Lai.

I am looking for job opportunity. Please drop me an email if you are interested.

I am a PhD student at Department of Computer and Information Sciences at University of Oregon. I am working with Thien Huu Nguyen in UofO Natural Language Processing group.

I obtained my M.S. degree in Computer Science from Japan Advanced Institute of Science and Technology (with Minh Le Nguyen) and bachelor degree in Computer Science from Posts and Telecommunications Institute of Science and Technology.

I interned at Adobe Research in Summer 2021 with Franck Dernoncourt. I work in projects involving video transcript understanding including: punctuation restoration, subtitle segmentation, and chitchat detection.

My research involves understanding of human languages that can be applied in computers to automate human-language cognitive tasks. Specifically, I focus on extracting and structurizing information from massive written text accross domains. My research topic lies in the intersection of Event Detection (ED) and Few-Shot Learning (FSL).
